How to Use the Plant Disease Predictor

  1. Select an image of your plant by clicking the "Choose an image" button.
  2. Once the image is uploaded, click the "Predict Disease" button.
  3. The application will analyze the image and display the result with possible disease names and treatment options.

If you encounter any issues or have questions, feel free to reach out through the Contact page.

Tips for Better Results

  • Ensure the image is clear and well-lit.
  • Capture the plant from multiple angles if possible.
  • Focus on areas showing symptoms of disease.
  • Try to avoid background distractions in your images.

Common Issues and Troubleshooting

Here are some common issues users may face and their solutions:

  • Image Not Uploading: Check your internet connection and ensure the file format is supported (e.g., JPG, PNG).
  • Analysis Taking Too Long: Please be patient; complex images may take a bit longer to process.
  • No Results Displayed: Ensure that the image quality is sufficient for analysis. Try uploading a different image if necessary.
